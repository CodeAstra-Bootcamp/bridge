Your First Job

What job would I get?

You will be good in software development by the end of the course.

We prepare students to be a junior developer. Depending on the company and exact role, developers are also called software developers, application developers, software engineers, software design engineers, or web developers. This job varies from company to company, but we define it as someone who is just beginning their developer career and is eager to learn, contribute, and grow. We encourage our junior developers to seek mentorship, whether through work or the development community.

You will continue to learn and grow at work.

Who hires me?

The demand for software developers is high in startups. As startups are growing exponentially, they would visit out campus for hiring. In addition, there will also be bigger and reputed corporations.

Salary?

The salary you get in your first job greatly depends on the amount of effort you put during the duration of the course. It also depends a little on your past academic and professional experirence.

We expect and guarantee a salary of at least 8 lakhs per annum. We failing the promise to provide you such offer withing 6 months of successful completion of the course would give you 100% tuition waiver; You need not pay the tuition.
